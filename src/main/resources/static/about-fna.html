About FNA

At FNA, our mission is to make the financial system safer and more efficient, and to play a pivotal role in the growing Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning ecosystem.

Our technology enables financial authorities, financial market infrastructures and financial institutions to successfully respond to the impact of local and systemic disruptions.

We do this by combining structured and unstructured data from a variety of sources to enable the visualization, analytics and monitoring of complex financial networks. We also help our clients optimise liquidity, and simulate various types of financial, operational, and conduct risks.

For learn more, please follow us on LinkedIn or visit www.fna.fi.
