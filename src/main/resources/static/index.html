Why FNA?

Financial Network Analytics (FNA) is a deep technology company and award-winning leader in Regulatory Technology (RegTech) and Supervisory Technology (SupTech). FNA combines industry leading data science capabilities with deep business domain expertise.

The FNA Platform allows financial regulators and FMIs to map and monitor complex financial networks and to simulate operational and financial risks. FNA’s clients include the world’s largest central banks, supervisors and financial market infrastructures.

Our work with G20

FNA’s software enables financial authorities, financial market infrastructures and financial institutions to successfully respond to the impact of local and systemic disruptions.

We believe that the FNA platform will provide added value to the work of financial authorities across the world especially at the time of unprecedented disruptions from the COVID-19 crisis. The FNA platform can facilitate stronger coordination amongst regulatory and supervisory agencies, and support them in formulating and implementing coordinated cross-jurisdictional responses.
